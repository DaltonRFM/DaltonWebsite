Welcome to the official website of Kappa Theta Pi - Nu Chapter at the University of Colorado at Boulder! If this is your first time hearing about us – we’re a professional technology fraternity on campus, driven by a mission to secure tech jobs for all our members. Joining KTP means gaining access to various resources designed to help you succeed in the tech industry. We offer a variety of workshops, academic support, community events, and hands-on projects to launch your career. You’ll immerse yourself in a community of peers who share your passion whatever that passion may be! We pride ourselves in our diverse community with interests spanning from quantitative finance to data science to biomedical engineering. We’re always looking to grow our membership! So many industries work closely with technology and there is no better time to get involved than today. Whether your passion lies in software engineering, design, or any other tech-related field, there’s a place for you at KTP. I encourage you to explore our website and Instagram to get to know our organization better. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us through a direct message or email, or arrange a coffee chat with any of our members. We want you to be part of our story and help us shape the future. I look forward to seeing you at our recruiting events! Wishing you nothing but the best and a warm welcome to KTP!
